Our Mission

Smart transport for modern world

Let’s Go is established on a straightforward idea that all populations be worthy of access to smart, affordable mobility. Through the reasonable distribution of shared scooters, we aim to reduce dependence on personal vehicles for quick move away transport and leave future generations with a fresher, healthier planet.

What moves you

Let's Go connects riders to the people and places that matter most, bringing cities together and empowering better urban living.

What makes us different

Truthfulness

Our distinct staff is committed to giving a safe, balanced micro-mobility experience in every city we serve.

Technology

Our drive to lead micro-mobility into the future puts Let’s Go on the cutting edge of clean, accessible urban transportation.

How it's work

Download the application

Download the free Let's Go app to rent our E-Scooter.

Locate

Use the Let’s Go app to find the closest Lime-S to you.

Scan

To unlock a scooter, simply scan the QR code located on the handlebars or the baseboard using the Let's Go app.

Ride

You're ready to go! Wherever your Let’s Go takes you, remember to obey traffic laws, to ride safely in the bike lane or street and to wear a helmet!
